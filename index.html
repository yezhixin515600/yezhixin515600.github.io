发表于 2021-12-17 |

Recently it was the summer holiday, and I had less homework than I did at school, so I had time to do something I liked. No matter in China or abroad, DIY is a popular project, which requires people to have a manipulative ability and creativity. During this holiday, I began to try DIY.
After some consideration, I chose to make my own phone case. But exactly I didn’t know how to do it, because in the past I just buy all my phone cases online. There was no idea in my mind, so I asked my classmates for help. She told me that I need to buy some accessories and cream glue. Cream glue was used for the base color, and accessories were used to decorate the phone case. Firstly, I needed to squeeze the cream glue onto the phone case and used tools to adjust the shape and color. And then, I needed to match all the materials which I wanted to use in my mind and also form a concept graph. It was a big challenge for me who had no experience. At first I just tried to search the Internet for some examples that someone else has already done, but soon I found that they were not what I wanted. And at the same time, I would loss the meaning of DIY. So I started to imagine my own combinations, trying out colors matching on blank paper and writing down each pair of combinations I liked. Moreover, I recorded the specific placement of decorations. And some of them were reworked by myself. For example, pasted the pearls around my drawing and stuck a bowknot at the bottom to disguise the feeling of the picture frame.
I had to say that creating something of my own gave me lots of sense of accomplishment. After making one phone case after another which was different from each other but completely belongs to me , I thought I fell in love with DIY and enjoyed every process of it. I had never thought I was such a creative person before.

BPHO competition experience

发表于 2021-07-21 |

When my teacher asked me if I was interested in participating the physical competition which called BPHO, I refused her. No other reasons, just because I don’t think I have the ability to attend this competition. Thanks to my patient and helpful teacher, she gave me a lot of information about this competition and also told me that teachers would teach us new knowledge to deal with the exam questions . So I didn’t need to be afraid. At last, after discussing with my mother, I decided to attend. Through a month of training，I got the bronze prize.
The fact prove that if you are willing to put your effort you will be rewarded. Before the examination，the school had arranged additional classes for all contestants at the weekend. And that means when others are having a rest, we need to prepare for the examination. Just for this reason, I missed a lot of chances to meet my friends. But whenever I thought that I had chosen to take the exam, I would encourage myself to persevere no matter what difficulties I met. Since I’ve made up my mind to attend BPHO, I couldn’t give up easily. Maybe I can win a prize because of my efforts. In this way, I persisted for a month. Although bronze wasn’t a very impressive prize, I was satisfied. Because in my point of view, my effort wasn’t paid off.

markdown的正文内容很好写，就按照一篇HTML文档写就可以了；但是不需要写那些样板代码，直接写普通文字或者我们想要嵌入的HTML标签和css样式即可。markdown文档的换行也和HTML是类似的，你敲一个换行符他会当做什么都没发生，想要换行你可以使用HTML的

Bruce 19 男
